Product Description

Overview

The Golden Tiger Luxury Designer Tote is a premium women's handbag crafted from 100% genuine full-grain cowhide leather, finished with hand-stitched reinforcement at all stress points. Designed to bridge functionality and high fashion, this bag seamlessly transitions from business meetings to social occasions.

Material & Construction

The exterior is constructed from Grade-A full-grain cowhide leather, tanned using an eco-certified vegetable process to preserve natural grain texture and aging characteristics. The interior is lined with Italian microfiber suede for a soft, scratch-resistant surface. All metal hardware — zippers, rings, and clasps — is crafted from solid zinc alloy with a 24K gold electroplated finish, tested to 5,000+ open/close cycles.

Design & Functionality

The multi-compartment architecture features one main zipper compartment, two open side pockets, a padded laptop sleeve (up to 13"), a built-in organizer panel with 6 card slots, a coin zipper pocket, and a key hook. The adjustable shoulder strap (100–120 cm) and padded top handles offer versatile carry options for all-day comfort.

Why Choose This Bag?

  • 100% genuine full-grain cowhide leather — not bonded or split leather
  • Eco-certified vegetable tanning process — durable, ages beautifully
  • 24K gold-plated zinc alloy hardware — tarnish and corrosion resistant
  • Hand-stitched reinforcement at all carry points — tested for 30 kg load
  • Anti-scratch microfiber interior lining — protects your belongings
  • Water-resistant nano-coating on exterior leather surface
  • Fits 13" laptop + A4 documents + daily essentials

Care Instructions

Wipe clean with a soft, dry cloth. Use leather conditioner every 3–6 months to maintain suppleness and color. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight or extreme humidity. Store in the provided dust bag when not in use.

Quality Control Process

60-Point Inspection
01
Incoming Material Inspection (IQC)
Every leather batch and hardware shipment is tested for color consistency, thickness, tensile strength, and chemical safety before production begins. Rejected rate: less than 0.5%.
02
In-Process Quality Control (IPQC)
QC inspectors stationed at each production stage: cutting, stitching, assembly, and edge finishing. Random sampling every 30 minutes per line.
03
Hardware & Zipper Testing
Zippers tested to 5,000+ open/close cycles. Hardware salt spray tested for 48 hours. Magnetic clasps tested for pull force ≥ 3 kg.
04
Load & Stress Testing
Handle and strap attachment points tested under 30 kg static load for 24 hours. Drop test from 1.2 meters onto all six sides.
05
Final Quality Inspection (FQI)
Every finished unit undergoes a 60-point visual and functional checklist covering stitching, hardware function, symmetry, cleanliness, and dimension accuracy (±3mm tolerance).
06
Third-Party Inspection Available
We welcome pre-shipment inspection by SGS, Bureau Veritas, TÜV, or your appointed inspector. Full cooperation and transparent access guaranteed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I order a sample before placing a bulk order?
Yes, we offer single samples at $55/piece including DHL shipping. The sample fee is fully refundable when you place a bulk order of 50+ pieces. Sample delivery takes 5–7 business days. Custom logo samples (with your branding) require an additional $30 setup fee and 7–10 days lead time.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for OEM/custom logo orders?
Standard embossed or hot-stamp logo requires a minimum of 50 pieces per color per style. Custom color combinations require 200 pieces minimum. Full bespoke new style development requires 500 pieces minimum. Small MOQ exceptions may be available for returning buyers — please discuss with our sales team.
How do I verify the leather is genuine and not PU or bonded leather?
We provide LWG (Leather Working Group) certification and third-party lab test reports confirming genuine full-grain cowhide origin. You can also request a flame test on sample swatches — genuine leather chars while PU melts. Our leather hides are sourced exclusively from certified tanneries in Henan and Zhejiang provinces with full traceability documentation.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept T/T (wire transfer), L/C (letter of credit) for orders over $10,000, Western Union, MoneyGram, and Trade Assurance through platform escrow. Standard terms are 30% deposit upfront and 70% balance before shipment. For long-term customers, Net 30 terms may be available after the third order.
Can you match a specific Pantone color for the leather?
Yes, we can color-match to any Pantone code or provide a physical swatch for matching. Color matching requires a minimum order of 200 pieces and adds 5–7 days to the production timeline. We send 3 test swatches for approval before full production. Note: a slight ±5% variance in leather dye is natural and expected.
What happens if I receive defective products?
We guarantee a defect rate below 0.3%. If you receive defective units, send us photos and a defect report within 14 days of delivery. We will replace defective units free of charge in your next shipment or issue a credit note. For large orders, we recommend engaging an independent pre-shipment inspector (SGS, BV, etc.) at your cost for additional assurance.
Do you offer drop-shipping services?
Yes, we offer blind drop-shipping for established buyers. Your branded packaging, invoices, and return labels can be used. There is no minimum quantity for drop-ship orders, but the unit price applies at the 1–9 pieces tier. A setup fee of $200 applies for initial drop-ship arrangement to cover packaging customization.
